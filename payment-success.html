What happens next

1. Watch for confirmation

A confirmation message may be sent to the email or phone number used for booking.

2. Prepare the dryer

Please keep the dryer accessible, remove clothes from inside, and clear space around it if possible.

3. Arrival window

The technician may arrive any time within your selected window, and the repair itself may extend beyond that window.

Service scope reminder

Dryer Dudes services free-standing electric dryers only. We do not service gas dryers, stacked units, washer-dryer combo units, or washers.

Rescheduling reminder

Rescheduling or cancellation needs to happen more than 48 hours before the appointment. Appointments cancelled or rescheduled within 48 hours of service are non-refundable.
