Common dryer problems we diagnose

Dryer not heating

A dryer that runs but does not heat may have an issue with the heating circuit, airflow, safety components, or related parts.

Dryer will not start

Starting issues may come from switches, door circuits, power issues, controls, or other electrical components.

Dryer takes too long

Long dry times often point to airflow problems, heating issues, clogged lint paths, or worn components.

Dryer making noise

Scraping, squealing, thumping, or rumbling can come from belts, rollers, idlers, or other moving parts.
