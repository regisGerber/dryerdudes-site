Before contacting us

Dryer Dudes only services free-standing electric dryers. The items below are outside the current service scope.

Not serviced

  • Gas dryers
  • Stacked units
  • Washer-dryer combos
  • Washers

Serviced

  • Free-standing electric dryers
  • Dryer not heating
  • Dryer not starting
  • Dryer noise
  • Long dry times
